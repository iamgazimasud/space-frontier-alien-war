Privacy Policy

Space Frontier: Alien War — last updated July 6, 2026

Summary

This game does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

Data stored on your device

Game progress (save slots, settings, achievements, and best scores) is stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and can be erased at any time by clearing the app's site data or uninstalling the app.

Network use

The app loads its game files from our web server over HTTPS and caches them for offline play. No personal information is sent in these requests beyond what any standard web request contains.

Children

The game is suitable for a general audience and collects no data from anyone, including children.
